PKCE Proxy

Below are the 3 URLs necessary for a PKCE OAuth flow. Use these in your client application in place of the original URLs that were provided by the OAuth service provider. Keep everything else the same.

These URLs contain your client ID and secret in an encrypted form and are therefore safe to distribute to unstrusted parties.

Authorize URL

https://PROXY_HOSTNAME/TOKEN/authorize


Token URL

https://PROXY_HOSTNAME/TOKEN/token


Refresh Token URL

https://PROXY_HOSTNAME/TOKEN/refresh-token
